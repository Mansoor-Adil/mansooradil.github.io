About Me

I am a Ph.D. student and Research Assistant at the RIDER Center at Florida State University, with a background in Civil Engineering and Remote Sensing & GIS. My work integrates geospatial analytics, machine learning, and sustainability science to address climate-related hazards and infrastructure resilience challenges.

I specialize in Web-GIS, spatial modeling, and predictive analytics, developing interactive dashboards, risk maps, and decision-support systems for disaster preparedness and response.
